About EasyPG

Your trusted platform for finding and managing PG accommodations

EasyPG is dedicated to simplifying the process of finding and managing PG accommodations for students and property owners. Our platform connects students with verified PG owners, providing a seamless experience from search to booking. We prioritize safety, transparency, and convenience, ensuring that both students and owners have a positive experience.

Founded in 2020, EasyPG has quickly become a trusted name in the accommodation industry, helping thousands of students find their ideal living spaces while empowering PG owners to manage their properties effectively. Our mission is to make PG accommodation search simple, secure, and hassle-free for everyone involved.
